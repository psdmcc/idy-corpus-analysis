[0.00-7.00]  Slowly bend to the right side and place the right hand just behind the right foot.
[7.00-11.00]  The left arm is straight up in line with the right arm.
[11.00-16.00]  Remain in this posture for 10 to 30 seconds with normal breathing.
[16.00-24.00]  This prevents flat foot. Strengthens the calf, thigh and waist muscles makes the spine flexible.
[24.00-28.00]  As you inhale, slowly come up.
[28.00-30.00]  Repeat for the left.
