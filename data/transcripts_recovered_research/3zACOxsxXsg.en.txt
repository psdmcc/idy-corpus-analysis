The information and broadcasting ministry of the Indian government celebrated international day of yoga on Saturday at the national media center in New Delhi. Secretary of information and broadcasting ministry Sanjay Jaju led the yoga session. The program was part of nationwide celebrations of international day of yoga with the theme yoga for one earth one health. Secretary Sanja Jaju highlighted the importance of yoga for physical and mental well-being.
