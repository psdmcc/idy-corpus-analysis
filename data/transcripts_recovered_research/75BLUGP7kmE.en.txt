On 20th June 2025, Arunoda International Residential School proudly celebrated International Yoga Day. The event began with a traditional lamp lighting followed by a soulful prayer song by our students. Our respected principal shared the importance of yoga highlighting its power to bring balance and discipline to life. A graceful Surya Namaskar demonstration was performed by our students, showcasing the essence of physical and mental harmony. Our chief guest, Mr. Binu Anaswara, a yoga teacher and lifestyle coach, delivered an inspiring message on making yoga a part of daily life. The celebration continued with a vibrant yoga presentation by our talented students and concluded with the national anthem. The day was a true celebration of peace, wellness, and unity through yoga. Happy International Yoga Day from Arunoda International Residential School. [Music]
