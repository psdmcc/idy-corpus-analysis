Tod ASA Todd means palm tree while doing this ASA your body posture becomes tall and strong like the palm [Music] tree to do this ASA first stand straight with both your feet at a distance of 2 in from one another now lift your hands till your shoulders keep your palms facing each other and then interlock your fingers together now slowly bend your wrists outwards now taking a deep breath in slowly lift your arms up above your head while keeping them in line with the shoulders keep your body steady in this position now slowly raise your heels up from the ground and stand on your toes without losing your balance or moving your feet stay in this position for about 10 to 15 seconds breathe in and out normally stay still in this posture it might be a little hard to maintain your balance in the beginning to help achieve this you can try focusing at a single point in front of your eyes doing this ASA regularly will help you improve your balance this ASA helps in getting rid of congestion in your spinal neres nerves it helps to improve the posture of the body and also strengthens your thighs knees and heels you should refrain from doing this ASA if you are suffering from any heart disease vertigo or varicose veins after staying in this position for 10 to 15 seconds slowly let your breath out and lower your heels back to the ground slowly release the interlock of the fingers bring the arms down and come back to the standing position practicing this ASA regularly bring stability to your body and mind
