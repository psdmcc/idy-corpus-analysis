And now with 25 days until the international day of yoga 2025, India's council general in Shangghai, Pratik Maturur met with Chinese yoga enthusiasts and Shu Day, president of China Asia Economic Development Association to promote yoga's holistic benefits. The Ministry of Aush praised the initiative, noting its role in fostering cultural exchange and health awareness. The event hosted by the Indian consulate in China featured discussions on yoga's global resonance aligning with the theme yoga for one earth one health. India's push reflects its leadership in yoga diplomacy since the UN declared June 21st as international day of yog in 2014 following Prime Minister Narendra Modi's advocacy.
